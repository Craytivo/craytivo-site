Privacy Policy

TL;DR: We're Craytivo Studio Inc., a web development studio. We collect contact info and project files to serve you, use analytics with consent, and never sell your data. We respect your privacy choices.

Table of Contents

1. Scope & Roles 2. What We Collect 3. How We Use Data & Legal Bases 4. Cookies & Tracking 5. Sharing & Processors 6. International Transfers 7. Retention 8. Your Rights 9. Security 10. Children 11. Third-Party Links 12. Changes 13. Contact

1. Scope & Roles

This Privacy Policy applies to our website and services. Craytivo Studio Inc. acts as a data controller for personal information collected through our website and marketing activities.

When we develop websites for clients, we may process end-user data as a data processor under our client's instructions. For client data processing agreements, please contact us at privacy@craytivo.com.

2. What We Collect

Contact Information

Name, email address, phone number, company name, and project details when you contact us or request a quote.

Project Files

Design files, content, and other materials you provide for website development projects.

Usage & Analytics

Website usage data, device information, and analytics (with your consent).

Server Logs

IP addresses, browser type, access times, and error logs for security and performance monitoring.

Payment Information

Payment details processed securely through Stripe (we don't store full payment information).

3. How We Use Data & Legal Bases

Contract Performance

To provide web development services, communicate about projects, and process payments.

Legitimate Interests

To improve our services, provide customer support, and send relevant business communications.

Consent

For optional analytics, marketing communications, and non-essential cookies.

Legal Obligation

To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

Note: If you're in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, we process your data based on these legal grounds as required by GDPR and UK data protection laws.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Essential Cookies

Required for website functionality, security, and basic performance monitoring.

Optional Cookies

Analytics, marketing, and preference cookies that require your consent.

Global Privacy Control

We respect the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal and will default to not tracking if enabled.

5. Sharing & Processors

We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our business:

Hosting: Vercel/Netlify for website hosting
Email: Google Workspace for business communications
Analytics: Plausible/Google Analytics (with consent)
Payments: Stripe for secure payment processing

All processors are bound by data processing agreements and may only use your data as instructed by us.

6. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EEA transfers
UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) for UK transfers
Adequacy decisions where applicable

Our primary hosting is in the United States, with additional processing in the EU and UK as needed.

7. Retention

Lead Information

24 months from last contact, then anonymized or deleted.

Project Files & Invoices

7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes.

Analytics Data

Maximum 14 months, with automatic deletion.

Server Logs

90 days for security monitoring, then deleted.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access your data
Correct inaccurate data
Delete your data
Data portability
Restrict processing
Opt-out of marketing

CPRA Notice: We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@craytivo.com.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

TLS encryption for all data transmission
Least privilege access controls
Encrypted storage and vendor systems
Regular security assessments
Incident response procedures

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We will notify you of any data breaches as required by law.

10. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date will be updated, and we will notify you of material changes via email or website notice.
